# 📊 Customer Acquisition Efficiency & Profit Quality Analysis

## 🎯 Objective

This project focuses on analyzing how efficiently a business acquires customers and how profitable those customers are over time. The goal is to evaluate cost effectiveness, identify high-value segments, and support better business decision-making.

---

## 📂 Dataset

The dataset includes customer transactions, acquisition costs, revenue, and profit-related metrics.

---

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies

* SQL
* Power BI

---

## 🔍 Key Analysis Performed

* Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC) analysis
* Revenue vs Acquisition Cost comparison
* Profit margin evaluation
* Customer segmentation based on profitability
* Trend analysis of customer value over time

---

## 📈 Key Insights

* High acquisition costs in certain segments reduce overall profitability
* A small group of customers contributes significantly to total revenue
* Some marketing strategies are cost-inefficient and need optimization
* Repeat customers generate higher profit compared to new customers

---

## 📊 Dashboard / Visualizations

* Revenue vs Cost comparison charts
* Profitability segmentation visuals
* Customer value distribution
* Trend analysis dashboards

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

* Focus on high-value customer segments
* Optimize marketing spend to reduce acquisition cost
* Improve retention strategies to increase repeat customers
* Eliminate low-performing acquisition channels

---

## 🚀 Conclusion

The analysis highlights the importance of balancing customer acquisition cost with long-term profitability. Businesses should prioritize efficient spending and customer retention to maximize overall profit quality.

This project demonstrates the ability to transform raw data into actionable business insights, helping organizations make data-driven decisions.
